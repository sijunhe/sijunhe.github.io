Looks like somebody has stumbled upon my blog!

My name is Sijun He. I am currently a machine learning engineer at [Twitter Cortex](https://cortex.twitter.com/en.html) focusing on Deep Learning and NLP for content understanding. Before that, I was a data scientist at [Autodesk](http://www.autodesk.com/), where I did analytics & data mining on products(i.e. [AutoCAD](https://www.autodesk.com/products/autocad/overview), [Revit](https://www.autodesk.com/products/revit-family/overview), [Maya](https://www.autodesk.com/products/maya/overview), etc.) that help engineers design [towering skyscrapers](https://www.autodesk.com/solutions/bim/hub/meet-shanghai-tower-chinas-tallest-building) and filemakers craft [Oscar-winning visual effects](http://usa.autodesk.com/adsk/servlet/item%3FsiteID%3D123112%26id%3D15300670).

The blog documents my journey of learning data science from scratch. It will mostly be reading notes and personal projects I have done for work, school or fun. **The blog is a journal for myself.** Whenever I am upset about not making enough progress (happens ALL THE TIME), I read my blog and remind myself of how far I have gone. If you like the blog, I encourage you to have one for yourself.
