Looks like somebody has stumbled upon my blog!

My name is Sijun He. I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Statistics at [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu/) while working as a data scientist at [Autodesk](http://www.autodesk.com/). 

In the summer of 2015, I graduated from [Rose-Hulman Institiute of Technology](http://www.rose-hulman.edu/) and was heading to Stanford for my graduate study in [Structural Engineering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Structural_engineering). Accidentally, I found my passion about data science and decided to make a career out of it. 

The blog documents my journey of learning data science from scratch. It will mostly be reading notes and personal projects I have done for work and school. The blog is a journal for myself. Whenever I am upset about not making enough progress (happens ALL THE TIME), I read my blog and remind myself of how far I have gone. If you like the blog, I encourage you to have one for yourself.
