About | Sijun He

Looks like somebody has stumbled upon my blog!

My name is Sijun He. I am a data scientist at Autodesk, where I mostly do analytics & machine learning on products(i.e. AutoCAD, Revit, Maya, etc.) that help engineers designing towering skyscrapers and filemakers crafting Oscar-winning visual effects.

In the summer of 2015, I graduated from Rose-Hulman Institiute of Technology and was heading to Stanford for my graduate study in Structural Engineering. Accidentally, I found my passion in data science and never looked back.

The blog documents my journey of learning data science from scratch. It will mostly be reading notes and personal projects I have done for work and school. The blog is a journal for myself. Whenever I am upset about not making enough progress (happens ALL THE TIME), I read my blog and remind myself of how far I have gone. If you like the blog, I encourage you to have one for yourself.
